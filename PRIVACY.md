# Privacy Policy

**Bundle** ("the Extension") is designed with your privacy in mind.

## Data Collection

The Extension **does not collect, store, transmit, or share** any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics to external servers.

## Local Processing

All image processing—including screenshots, cropping, full-page capture, and color picking—is performed **entirely on your device** using Chrome's Offscreen Document API.

**No data leaves your computer.**

## Permissions Usage

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Required to capture screenshots of the current tab |
| `downloads` | Required to save captured images to your device |
| `storage` | Used only to remember your theme preference (light/dark) |
| `offscreen` | Required for canvas-based image processing |
| `scripting` | Required to inject UI elements into web pages |
| `alarms` | Required to keep the extension service worker alive |

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

## Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
